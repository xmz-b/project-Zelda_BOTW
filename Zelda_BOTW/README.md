# Project Zelda-BOTW

## Characters

### Abilities explanation

#### Link
-Remote bombs: spherical/cubic
-Magnesis: magnetic manipulation
-Stasis: time-freezing (on objects)
-Cryonis: ice pillars

#### Zelda
-sealing: the ability to seal Ganon away

#### Calamity Ganon
-posses/corrupt guardians: Guardians are a result of ancient technology made by the sheikah to protect Hyrule.
-reviving, reanimating, empowering monsters: With the use of malice.

#### Mipha
-Mipha's Grace: Fully restores links health when he dies, the ability has a 24-minute cooldown.

#### Revali
-Revali's Gale:  It creates a powerful, localized upward draft of wind, enabling Link to launch into the air for easier traversal. It has 3 charges with a 6-minute cooldown (2 minutes in Hyrule Castle). 

#### Urbosa
-Urbosa's Fury: It acts as a charged spin attack that drops a dome of lightning on surrounding enemies, dealing high damage (150-500) and stunning them. It has three charges with a 12-minute recharge time. 

#### Daruk
-Daruk's Protection: creates a red, spherical energy shield around Link when holding the ZL button to guard. It automatically parries enemy attacks, including Guardian beams, and has 3 charges that replenish over 18 minutes.

#### Malanya
-reviving: reviving dead horses

#### Hestu
-Inventory Expansion: Hestu accepts Korok Seeds collected throughout Hyrule to increase the number of slots in Link's Weapon, Bow, or Shield stashes. Each category can be upgraded multiple times, requiring progressively more seeds (1, 2, 3, etc.) for each subsequent expansion, up to a maximum of 441 seeds to fully expand all slots.

#### Master Kohga
-teleportation: Master Kohga can teleport himself anywhere.
-levitation: Master Kohga can levitate iron.
-conjuring: Master Kohga can conjure massive spiked iron balls.
